About Me

I was born and raised in California and currently live in Nevada. I am Mexican Japanese and French and I love mexican and asian food. I have a chocolate labrador named Jupiter. I enlisted into the Marine Corps as a Field Artillery Cannon Crewman and traveled around the US and Japan while I was in the military. I completed my four year contract and went to school for aviation maintenance and now I am currently enrolled in UNLV for software development.
